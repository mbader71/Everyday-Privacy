Frequently Asked Questions

How do I take my daily selfie?

Open the app and tap the Capture tab. A faint outline of your previous photo appears to help you line up the shot, then tap the shutter.

How do I set or change my daily reminder?

Go to the Settings tab and open Daily Reminder. You can turn the reminder on or off, choose a fixed time, or pick a random time within a range.

Can I import selfies I already have?

Yes. During setup, or later from Settings → Import from Photo Library, you can select existing photos to add to your timeline.

How do I create a timelapse video?

Open Settings → Timelapse, choose a quality level, and tap Generate Timelapse Video. You can then save it to your Photo Library or share it.

Does my data sync across devices?

Yes. If you're signed in to iCloud, your timeline syncs privately across your Apple devices through iCloud. The app works fully even without iCloud.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Everyday has no servers and no tracking — your photos stay on your device and in your own iCloud account. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete a photo?

Deleting a selfie from your iOS Photo Library removes it from the app's timeline.
